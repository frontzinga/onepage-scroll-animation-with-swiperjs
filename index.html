Tomura Shigaraki

Tomura possesses a unique Quirk called "Decay." With this power, he can disintegrate anything he touches, causing it to crumble and turn to dust. His Quirk also extends to living beings, allowing him to kill with a single touch. He often uses his Quirk to destroy structures and objects as a symbol of his anarchic ideology.

As the leader of the League of Villains, Tomura Shigaraki is ruthless, sadistic, and harbors a deep desire to bring chaos and destruction to society. He is driven by his hatred for heroes and seeks to dismantle the current hero system. Despite his volatile nature, Tomura is intelligent and manipulative, capable of devising intricate plans to further his goals.

Throughout the series, Tomura Shigaraki undergoes character development and faces various challenges that shape his ideology and aspirations. His character arc explores themes of identity, trauma, and the corrupting influence of power.

Dabi

Dabi has a distinctive appearance that reflects his villainous nature. He is a tall and lean young man with messy, unkempt, and scruffy blue hair that covers his left eye. His skin is pale, and he has numerous burn scars covering a significant portion of his body, including his face, torso, and arms. Dabi typically wears tattered and ragged clothing, giving him a disheveled and rebellious look.

Dabi's Quirk is called "Cremation." It allows him to generate and manipulate blue flames, which burn at an extremely high temperature. He can produce intense fire blasts, create walls of flames, and engulf his opponents in searing fire. Dabi's Quirk is especially dangerous as it can incinerate objects and people, making him a formidable adversary in battle.

Throughout the series, Dabi plays a significant role in the League of Villains' plans and acts as a key antagonist in various story arcs. His character arc delves into themes of trauma, identity, and the consequences of a broken system.

Himiko Toga

In terms of personality, Himiko Toga is depicted as eccentric and mentally unstable. She has an intense fascination with blood and a strong desire to experience the essence of others. Himiko is infatuated with the idea of becoming her chosen person, idolizing them to the point of obsession. This obsession often drives her actions and influences her methods as a villain.

Himiko possesses a unique Quirk called "Transform." This Quirk allows her to assume the appearance, voice, and some aspects of the blood of a person she has consumed. By ingesting someone's blood, she can transform into a perfect replica of that person, mimicking their physical appearance and abilities to some extent. This ability also grants her some knowledge of the person she transforms into, further aiding her deception.

Throughout the series, Himiko Toga plays a significant role in the League of Villains' activities. She is known for her unpredictability, often using her shapeshifting abilities to infiltrate and manipulate situations to her advantage. Himiko is portrayed as a chaotic and dangerous individual, reveling in violence and bloodshed.
